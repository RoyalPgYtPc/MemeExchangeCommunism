★ EVENT DOCUMENTATION

10M Milestone Giveaway Terms

Official rules and participation guidelines for the 10 Million Visitors Celebration & Giveaway event.

Event Duration

7 Days (Starts from Announcement Date)

Prizes

Meme Currency (MC) & Bonus Multipliers

Authority

r/MemeExchangeCommunism Moderators

1. The 7-Day Bonus Event

To celebrate our 10M visitor milestone, the system is rolling out tiered bonuses for all community contributors:

  • Initial Phase (2 Days): All eligible users receive a flat MC bonus multiplier on all standard exchange activities.
  • Second Phase (5 Days): A follow-up bonus structure will be applied for the remainder of the event week.
  • Automatic Application: Bonuses are applied server-side. Users do not need to manually activate multipliers unless specified in the bot interface.

2. Giveaway Eligibility

Participation in the "Meme Currency Giveaway" is open to users who meet the following criteria at the time of entry:

  • Active membership in r/MemeExchangeCommunism.
  • Account must be older than 30 days to prevent sybil attacks/botting.
  • No history of major community violations or active bans within the last 90 days.

3. Entry Requirements

Valid entries must follow the instructions provided in the official announcement post. Standard entry methods may include:

  • Commenting with specific keywords or feedback.
  • Engaging with community achievements as tracked by the bot.
  • Compliance with any "Join Discord" or "Elections" related requirements mentioned in the event thread.

4. Prize Distribution & Limitations

Winning entries are selected at the sole discretion of the moderation team or via a randomized verifiable bot. Prizes are strictly virtual:

  • Winners will receive Meme Currency (MC) deposited directly to their bot balance.
  • Prizes carry no monetary value and are subject to the existing Meme Currency EULA regarding usage and limits.
  • The moderation team reserves the right to withhold prizes if suspicious activity or "alt-farming" is detected.

5. Modifications and Cancellation

The r/MemeExchangeCommunism moderation team reserves the right to cancel, suspend, or modify the giveaway at any time due to technical issues, fraud, or unforeseen exploits that threaten the integrity of the Meme Currency system.

Notice

This document serves as an addendum to the standard Meme Currency EULA specifically for the 10M Milestone Event. By participating, you acknowledge that all decisions made by the r/MemeExchangeCommunism moderators are final and binding.
